Feature Article

Why Managing Screen Time Matters

Spending too much time on devices can affect your sleep, schoolwork, and relationships. Learning to balance screen time is essential for your mental and physical wellbeing.

Advice Column

“Am I Online Too Much?”

Many teens ask: “What is the Wellbeing WiFi?” It’s about knowing when to stay connected and when to unplug. Start with small steps—set limits, turn off notifications, and take breaks.

  • Use apps that track your time online
  • Try “no phone zones” at meals
  • Replace 30 mins of scrolling with a hobby

Creative Corner

Here’s a creative way to think about digital wellbeing: Imagine your phone as a toolbox. Every app is a tool—some help you grow, others waste your time. Which tools will you choose to use wisely?
